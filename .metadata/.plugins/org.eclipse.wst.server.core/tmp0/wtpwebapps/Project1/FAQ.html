Frequently Asked Questions


Orders and Tracking


Orders usually take 7-14 days. But they may take over 14 days depending on delivery location.

If order has already been shipped, you cannot cancel the order. Otherwise try the cancel button under your order.

Currently we do not allow changes in shipping address once the shipping process has started.

Currently we do not allow cancellation of products that have been shipped. Orders at previous levels may be cancelled.

Returns and Exchange


Currently we do not allow exchanges in products, but it will be soon implemented wherein orders can be exchanged within a period of 10 days from delivery. However, a refund policy will not be implemented.

e-mailed updates from Delivery companies as well as from us will keep you posted
